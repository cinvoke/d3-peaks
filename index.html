Understanding Peak Finding

March 17, 2016 by Efe Karakus

In this article, I explain how the d3-peaks plugin works. The plugin is based on the work of Du, Pan, Warren A. Kibbe, and Simon M. Lin. "Improved peak detection in mass spectrum by incorporating continuous wavelet transform-based pattern matching." Bioinformatics 22.17 (2006): 2059-2065.

The goal of this article is not to only explain how the algorithm works. But, hopefully achieve a higher goal and show that with examples and explanations we can make complex research papers a lot more readable, and hence reach a broader audience.

# Problem

Imagine we are given the heart rates of an individual while exercising. We want to find out the periods of rest and activity. By finding relevant peaks in the dataset, we can discern the underlying region beneath it and label the regions accordingly.
